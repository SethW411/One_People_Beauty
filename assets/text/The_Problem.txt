What is the problem we will be solving?

1. Moisture Retention:
Curly, wavy, and coily hair types tend to have a more porous structure due to the natural shape of the hair strands. This makes it easier for moisture to escape and hair to become dry and frizzy.

2. Growth:
Curly, wavy, and coily hair can appear to grow more slowly compared to straight hair because of the coil pattern. The natural curls can make it challenging to notice length changes.

3. Performance:
When hair doesn't perform as desired, it can lead to styling difficulties, loss of curl definition, and the need for frequent touch-ups or restyling.
